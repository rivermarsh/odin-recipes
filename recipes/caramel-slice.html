Caramel Slice

Description

Caramel slice is terrific. I chose this recipe because the it had the thickest layer of caramel in the google images search

Ingredients

  • 250g malt biscuits
  • 390g condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar firmly packed
  • 90g butter
  • 2 tbs golden syrup
  • 200g chocolate melts

Steps

  1. Lightly grease a 27 cm slice container with cooking spray. Line bottom with approximately 15 whole biscuits. Crush the remaining biscuits.
  2. In a large saucepan place the left over crushed biscuits, condensed milk, butter, golden syrup and brown sugar.
  3. Mix together over medium heat, stirring until mixture begins to boil.
  4. Reduce heat to low, stirring all the time to unsure mixture doesn't stick to bottom of pan.
  5. After about 8-10 minutes the mixture will turn a rich brown colour and will begin to separate from the side of the saucepan.
  6. Pour caramel over the biscuit base.
  7. Melt chocolate and spread over caramel.
  8. Refrigerate to set.
