Cheese Scones

Description

I found this on foodlovers.co.nz. I haven't tried this particular recipe before, but as long as you drown it in butter i'm sure even a mediocre cheese scone is still pretty good

Ingredients

  • 2 rounded cups of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 50g butter
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1-2 cupts grated tasty cheese
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 C
  2. Sift the flour and salt into a mixing bowl. Rub in the butter until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs
  3. Stir through the baking powder and then the cheese
  4. Make a well in the centre and add milk, mixing quickly with a knife to form a soft dough
  5. Tip dough onto a lightly floured board and pat or roll into a rectangle.
  6. Cut into shapes and brush with a little milk
  7. Place on a baking tray and cook for 12-15 minutes until a rich golden colour
